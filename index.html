PROFESSIONAL BIOGRAPHY

I'm a Data Science and AI postgraduate at the University of Liverpool, currently working on a thesis focused on predictive models for stock trading. Before diving into machine learning, I earned my ACCA qualification with AA prizewinner honors, developing deep experience in financial analysis and auditing for international clients managing multi-million dollar portfolios.

My work lives at the intersection of finance and AI—where hard numbers meet smart algorithms. But I’m also driven by a broader ambition: to build systems that are not just intelligent, but sustainable. I'm an advocate for a solarpunk future—where technology harmonizes with nature to support both human well-being and environmental resilience.

This portfolio highlights my projects in machine learning, financial modeling, and data visualization, alongside writing that explores the overlap between technology, ecology, and society. For me, data science isn’t just about optimization, it’s about shaping smarter, fairer, and greener futures.
